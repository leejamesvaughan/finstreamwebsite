Self-Book Appointments

Let customers see your availability and self-book their own appointments. Focus less time on scheduling and more on what really moves the needle with your customers.

  • Finstream automatically reminds customers of upcoming appointments via in-app notifications and push messages.

  • In the age of Covid, give your customers the additional option to book either an in-person meeting, or a Zoom video chat.

  • Set custom time allocations depending on the meeting type.

  • Finstream integrates with either your Google or Outlook calendar for a seamless scheduling experience.

Customer Receipts

Finstream let’s customers easily record business receipts by either using their camera phone, or by uploading receipts manually.  All receipts are stored in the cloud and our inbuilt Aritificial Intelligence engine will scan and document the GST and receipt cost automatically.

  • Give your customers a easy way to store business related expenses.

  • Save your customers money. No need for customers to run expensive receipt tracking software.

  • Easy export option for pain-free collaboration and less paperwork.

Communication

Tired of your inbox being overloaded with messages?
Finstream allows you to either directly send in-app messages or SMS your customers. We’ll save you from inbox overload.

  • Reduce your inbox dependency and all the back and forth emails with customers.

  • No restrictions on the number of messages sent.

Give Homework, Set Tasks

Prepare your customers by sending them pre-meeting tasks and give document requests post meeting. With Finstream you can bypass all the annoying back and forth requests and streamline collaboration between you and your customers.

  • Set pre-meeting tasks and ensure your customer is organised prior to their meeting.

  • After your meeting, give your customers homework items to complete.

  • Clearer requirements and a linear customer journey that stays out of your inbox.

Keep Customers In The Loop

Easily notify your customers of any progress made in their tax return. No more second guessing progress or miscommunication. Finstream allows you to be completly transparent for a better customer journey.

  • Notify customers of when you have completed and submitted their tax return.

  • Notify customers of what their tax return is once returned from the ATO.
